Our story

GhostDirective was founded in 2024 by Sir Reginald McFakename III with a simple mission: what if we could actually understand what animals are saying?

Starting with penguins — one of the most vocal and social species on Earth — we're building the foundation for cross-species communication. Based in Reykjavik, Iceland, our team of 12 researchers and engineers is working to make interspecies understanding a reality.
